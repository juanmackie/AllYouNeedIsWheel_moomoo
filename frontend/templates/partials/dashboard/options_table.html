What Are These Options Strategies?

Covered Calls: You own 100+ shares of stock and sell the right for someone else to buy them at a set price. You collect a premium upfront. Best for generating income from stocks you already own.

Cash-Secured Puts: You sell the right for someone to sell you their shares at a set price. You need cash in your account to "secure" this promise. Best for buying stocks you want at lower prices.

Column Guide
OTM %: Distance from current price (higher = safer but less premium)
Strike: Price where option activates
Expiration: When the option expires (DTE = Days to Expiration)
Mid Price: Average of buy/sell price you should aim for
Delta: Chance option finishes profitable (0-1 scale)
IV%: Implied Volatility - market's price movement expectation
Qty: Number of contracts (100 shares each)
Total Premium: Income you'll receive immediately
Cash Required: For puts - money held as collateral
