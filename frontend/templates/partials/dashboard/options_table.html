Strategy context

Covered Calls: Generate income from shares you already own by selling the right for someone else to buy them at a set price.

Cash-Secured Puts: Collect premium while setting aside enough cash to buy shares if assigned at your chosen strike.

Reading the score

  • 80+ High-quality opportunity
  • 65-79 Good opportunity
  • <65 Fair, review warnings
  • IV 70%+ Premium-rich environment
  • IV <30% Lower premium environment
OTM % controls the distance from current price. Higher values reduce assignment risk but also reduce premium.
Delta approximates the probability an option finishes in the money. Lower generally means more buffer.
Total Premium and Cash Required help you compare income against capital committed.
